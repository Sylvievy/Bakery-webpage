La Forêt Chocolatée

Welcome to La Forêt Chocolatée, where nature inspires chocolate perfection since 1995. Located in Bellefeuille, our artisanal bakery crafts exquisite chocolates that capture the essence of the French countryside. Indulge in our truffles and pralines, each a taste of pure delight!



History

La Forêt Chocolatée" was born in the quaint village of Bellefeuille in the picturesque French countryside in 1995. Founded by the visionary chocolatier, Émilie Duval, it began as a modest chocolate shop nestled amidst the enchanting forests of the region. Inspired by the abundance of nature and her passion for chocolate craftsmanship, Émilie created exquisite chocolate delicacies that captured the hearts of locals and tourists alike. Over the years, "La Forêt Chocolatée" has remained a cherished destination, blending tradition with innovation to offer a tantalizing journey through the flavors of the forest.
